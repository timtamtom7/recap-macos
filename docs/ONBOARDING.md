# Recap — Onboarding

4 screens for the meeting recorder.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Recap

**Headline:** Never miss a moment.

**Body:** Recap records your meetings with screen, audio, and timestamps — so you can focus on the conversation, not the notes. Everything is saved locally, privately, on your Mac.

**Illustration:** A flat, modern illustration of a laptop screen showing a video call grid. A red "REC" dot is visible in the corner. Above the laptop, floating timestamp markers (small purple diamonds) drift upward like notes being captured. Clean dark background with subtle blue and red accents.

**CTA Button:** "Start Setup →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right)

---

## Screen 2 — Permissions Needed

**Headline:** Just a few quick permissions.

**Body:** Recap needs access to your screen and microphone to record meetings. All recordings stay on your Mac — we never upload anything.

**Illustration:** A friendly illustration showing a Mac screen with 3 permission dialog boxes (Screen Recording, Microphone, Files & Folders) in a stacked layout. Each dialog has a green checkmark overlay. A small lock icon in the corner. Friendly, non-intimidating style.

**CTA Button:** "Grant Permissions"

**Secondary note:** Small text: "You can change these anytime in System Settings → Privacy & Security"

---

## Screen 3 — How It Works

**Headline:** Record, mark, share.

**Body:** Press `⌘⇧R` to start recording. During the meeting, press `M` to drop a timestamp marker on an important moment. When you're done, review, trim, and export — or share a direct link to the recording.

**Illustration:** A 3-step horizontal flow illustration:
1. Left: A keyboard with ⌘⇧R highlighted, a red dot appears above it (Record)
2. Center: A waveform timeline with a purple diamond marker being dropped (Mark)
3. Right: A play button and export/share icon (Review)

Each step connected by arrows. Minimal, clean.

**CTA Button:** "Next →"

---

## Screen 4 — Ready to Record

**Headline:** You're all set.

**Body:** Press `⌘⇧R` anytime to start recording. Your first recording will appear here.

**Illustration:** A celebratory but clean illustration: a Recap window open showing an empty recordings library, with a large "Start Recording" button highlighted in red. A small red dot pulses gently in the corner. A subtle checkmark badge in green. Professional, not gimmicky.

**CTA Button:** "Open Recap"
